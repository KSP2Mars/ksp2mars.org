--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About KSP2Mars is the third iteration of a community mission to land a Kerbal on the surface of Mars, in the game Kerbal Space Program. Mods will be used to make the game more realistic - Kerbals have to be supplied with food, water and oxygen; realistic fuels and aerodynamics as well as accurate planet scaling must be taken into account, among other things. We also have a [blog](https://blog.ksp2mars.org) where we publish our newsletter, a [subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/KSP2Mars) for general discussion, a [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCJIG7sqvEvjBvmvsKXMvkJA) for our videos and a [Twitter](https://twitter.com/ksp2mars) for what we're up to.
